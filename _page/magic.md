## Competitive Magic

I began playing Magic during 10th Edition and started competing seriously during Return to Ravnica.

Achievements:
- Three Pro Tour appearances
- World Championship appearance
- Regional Championship Top 8

Competitive Magic has taught me how environments evolve, how players solve formats, and how small design decisions affect entire metagames.
